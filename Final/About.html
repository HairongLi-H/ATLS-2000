Hi, my name is Hairong Li, I'm currently a senior student study in University of Colorado, Boulder.

Design is omnipresent. How things are designed and built affects our daily lives in the most minuscule and broadest of forms. For this reason, I passionately believe that it is our responsibility as designers to not only uphold a certain quality of life standard but also to keep finding creative ways to keep finding creative ways to better enhance the human experience both indoors and outdoors. I aim to be a steward of the environment by integrating the knowledge and skills I have acquired in my undergraduate studies into my professional career, helping to create spaces and communities that are both resilient and socially equitable. Through thoughtful design and planning, I desire to cultivate places, experiences, and conditions that are impactful to people's wellbeing and their surroundings.
